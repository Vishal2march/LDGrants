TO:         Reviewers of NYS Conservation/Preservation Coordinated Grant Contracts

 

FROM:   Barbara Lilley        

 

 

 

Before you begin your evaluations, you should read the Conservation/ Preservation Coordinated Preservation Grant Program Guidelines document carefully. 

 

Two independent conservators, archivists or preservation administrators are being asked to review each proposal. Since conservation/ preservation is a small field and it is difficult (often impossible) to assemble a panel of competent people who have no contact with the applicants, the question of "conflict of interest" may arise as you look over the applications. If you feel that you cannot be impartial about a proposal, please contact me immediately so we can decide if we need to send the proposal to another reviewer.

 

The grant review form will explain how you are to rate the various sections of the proposals.  You will rate the individual components of the project.  Once we have tabulated the three reviewers' (including program staff) ratings, the proposals are ranked, based on composite scores. You may complete the rating form online and submit the evaluation through the Division of Library Development Online Grant System.

 

You are strongly encouraged to make narrative comments on each proposal. Narrative comments have proved to be very helpful in responding to applicants' requests for information about how their proposals were reviewed, and how future applications might be improved.  Your comments may be as expansive as time will allow.

 

As those of you who have been reviewers in the past know, application and review procedures have evolved considerably.  To help us make further improvements, you are encouraged to comment on the program, the application and review process, and any issues that may require a change in policy or should be addressed in the guidelines.

 

If you need help with the review procedure or in interpreting the guidelines, please feel free to call me.  Our number is (518) 474-7890.  Thanks very much for agreeing to participate in this year's review.  I appreciate your contribution of time and expertise and hope that you will find the process interesting and useful.

 

All evaluations should be submitted to our office in a timely manner.
